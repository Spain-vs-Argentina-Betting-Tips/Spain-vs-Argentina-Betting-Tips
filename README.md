## Staking Discipline Across The Tiers

The whole point of tiers is to size stakes by risk. Apply this framework and the guide does its job:

- **Safe tier:** 1–2 units per selection. Highest hit rate, biggest stake.
- **Balanced tier:** 0.5–1 unit. Fair value, moderate stake.
- **Longshot tier:** 0.1–0.25 unit. Upside only — never a bankroll bet.
- **One unit = 1–2% of bankroll**, fixed. Do not flex it on gut feel.
- **Never chase.** A losing longshot is expected. Move on and stay flat.

Bankroll discipline is what separates a repeatable **betting strategy** from gambling on vibes. The tier that fits you is the one that matches your budget and temperament — respect it, and let flat staking protect you from the variance that a game this tight guarantees.
